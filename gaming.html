What are my favourite Genres

1. FIGHTING GAMES

Fighting games are amazing. They seem so simple yet are so complex in nature. The mechanics you have to learn, neutral play, defense, attacking, even the combos, there's such a beautiful layer of complexity that lures me in to it. My favourite fighting game is Street Fighter III 3rd Strike, a gem of a fighting game, especially complimented by evo moment 37.

2. RPGs

RPGs will remain a staple of gaming. They always boast amazing story, insane worlds to uncover and explore, and my favourite, linear progression. The facet that I can dictate how my character grows in strength over time is amazing. That being said, my favourite RPG is a whole different breed. Dark Souls, the game I instantly fell in love with. There are 3 reasons I love this game; the overarching story is amazing; the world, all the bosses, NPCs and the level up system; and finally, my favourite part of it, THE DIFFICULTY.

3. Pokemon (Yes it deserves its own Genre)

I never thought I would be one to like pokemon but it really grew on me. I discovered it when a friend on discord recommended it to me. The game he recommended was Pokemon Revolution Online. So the first Pokemon game i've ever played was an online MMO. From there i learned the mechanics, the story, and just fell in love with it. I then started to play the DS games, starting with Pokemon White. Heart Gold has been my fave ever since. And this leads me into:
